About The Role

Four Seasons New York is seeking a People & Culture Manager who embodies a passion for excellence and demonstrates unwavering enthusiasm in fostering a positive, service-oriented culture. The ideal candidate will inspire and engage employees at all levels, ensuring the highest standards of service and employee care are consistently upheld. This role requires a collaborative leader with exceptional interpersonal skills, a commitment to nurturing talent, and the ability to cultivate an inclusive and motivating workplace environment.

What You Will Do

    Conduct interviews and evaluate candidate for line-level and management roles
    Manage and oversee employee leave of absence programs and workers' compensation
    Administer employee benefits and ensure timely enrollment and eligibility tracking.
    Foster positive, professional relationships with all employees
    Maintain and manage employee data within Workday and ADP systems
    Actively participate in Embark, new hire orientation program
    Uphold a high level of professionalism and integrity while ensuring strict confidentiality of People & Culture matters

What You Bring

    2-3 years of Human Resources experience, preferably in employment and benefits
    Strong recruitment knowledge
    Bachelor's degree in human resources or related field preferred
    Prior New York and union experience is preferred

What We Offer

    Competitive salary and a comprehensive benefits package
    Market-leading pay and benefits (Medical, Dental, Vision and Retirement Savings Plan)
    Complimentary accommodation at other Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts
    Complimentary employee meals
    Paid holidays, vacation, and sick days
    Culinary, retail and wellness experiences at special rates

Schedule & Hours

This role is a full time, on-site role.

Compensation

80,000 - 83,000 Annual Salary
